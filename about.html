our story who are we?

ACTIVeat is Mumbai’s premium online healthy meal delivery service.

We at ACTIVeat are Foodies, Chefs and Dieticians who are committed to providing you nutritious and delicious meals at your convenience.

There is no one size fits all when it comes to Meal prep, so at ACTIVeat we design meals to aide your long term health and fitness goals whether it is weight loss, muscle gain or weight maintenance.

With all your meals cooked and delivered, you can spend more time doing the things you love, all while eating delicious healthy meals and reaching your goals!
